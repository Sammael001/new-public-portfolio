Project Showcase: Office Dashboard

The Office Dashboard app suite was built to resolve issues with inter-office communications and logistics in Customer Care.

Front-end: Javascript and EJS

Server: NodeJS and ExpressJS

Database: MongoDB Atlas and Mongoose

Host: Heroku

The Office Dashboard app suite was developed for my current employer and is currently in use by their Customer Care department.

The apps are written in Javascript with EJS templates, with NodeJS/ExpressJS for server-side scripting, MongoDB/Mongoose for database operations and PassportJS for secure user authentication and serialization.

During development I received guidelines, feedback and app requirements from Netscout's Director of Technical Support, Director of Sales and Director of Technical Operations.

The suite includes the following:

  • An app to track warehouse inventory
  • An app to share inter-office memos and policy updates
  • An app to locate sales representatives by state, province or country
  • An app to identify part numbers by product serial
